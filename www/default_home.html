Multi-Omics Network Explorer
An interface to explore data from Genomics Epigenomics Transcriptomics Proteomics Metabolomics associations.
Different omics layers are integrated into interpretable molecular subnetworks. Explore molecules, identify neighbors.
What You Can Do
  • Upload multi-omics association or correlation files.
  • Build multi-layer networks across platforms.
  • Explore trait-specific neighborhoods interactively.
  • Customize node shapes and colors.
  • Export subnetworks and tables.
  • Save and reload custom views.
Quick Start
  • 1. Check required formats in Data Formats.
  • 2. Upload your excel file via Import Data.
  • 3. Explore excel sheets in Tables.
  • 4. Visualize networks in Network.
  • 5. Export & save views from Export and Saved-views.
